Domestic Flights

Chittagong

Chittagong is the Business Capital of Bangladesh, situated in southeastern part of the country. This divisional city is the biggest seaport of Bangladesh and is on the banks of the Karnaphuli River on the Bay of Bengal.

Barishal

Barishal is the biggest river port in Bangladesh. Situated on the Kirtankhola river and on the northern shore of the Bay of Bengal in southern part of Bangladesh. The best attraction of Barishal is Kuakata sea beach.

Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is the tourism capital of Bangladesh, it is known for its wide sandy beach, which is the world's longest unbroken natural sandy sea beach, which is 125 km long with a gentle slope.

Jessore

Jessore, is a district town located in the Khulna Division of southwestern part of Bangladesh. It is bordered by India to the west. One of the oldest municipalities of the country, Jashore town on the Bhairab river.

Rajshahi

Rajshahi city located in the north-west of the country. Rajshahi known as the Silk City as it producing the best & purest silk in the country, also known as Education City or the City of Mango of Bangladesh.

Saidpur

Saidpur is a city located in Nilphamari district in Rangpur division and having the northernmost airport in country. Saidpur is a very important communication hub for adjoining major district headquarters.
